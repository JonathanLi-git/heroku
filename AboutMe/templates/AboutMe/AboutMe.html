More about Me

I grew up in a family of engineers. My father, mother both mechanical engineers and my brother a computer scientist. Because of my background, I've always seen things with an engineer's eye. For the work I do and in day to day life, I always notice the little details and strive to set a high standard for myself. However, being brought up see things with a systematic approach cause my communication and social skills to lack behind of the kids my age.
